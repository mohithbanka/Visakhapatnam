About Visakhapatnam

Visakhapatnam, also known as Vizag, is a picturesque coastal city in Andhra Pradesh, India. Nestled between the Eastern Ghats and the Bay of Bengal, it is renowned for its beaches, rich cultural heritage, and bustling port. It is also a hub for education, tourism, and industry.

Top Attractions

  • RK Beach: A serene spot for relaxation and evening strolls.
  • Kailasagiri: A hilltop park offering panoramic views of the city.
  • Borra Caves: Natural limestone caves with stunning formations.
  • Araku Valley: A scenic hill station with coffee plantations and waterfalls.
  • Submarine Museum: A unique museum housed in a decommissioned submarine.

Local Food

Vizag is famous for its delicious seafood and traditional Andhra cuisine. Don't miss out on:

  • Pulasa Fish Curry
  • Andhra Chicken Biryani
  • Gongura Pachadi
  • Chepala Pulusu (Fish Curry)
